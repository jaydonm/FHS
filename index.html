For Humanity's Sake is a 501-C-3 non-profit organization that operates in the Cape Verdean Island of São Nicolau. For Humanity's Sake provides educational and nutritional resources to needy students and their families. We believe that sustainable impact starts by creating a better future for the disadvantaged. Our organization dedicates its efforts to advancing the personal development of the children in São Nicolau with access to education. We believe that education is the foundation for building a happy and healthy life.
